About Ravan Dahan Dusshera WRS

Ravan Dahan at Water Resources Department is a grand event celebrated during Dusshera in Raipur. Join us as we mark the victory of good over evil with the symbolic burning of the effigy of Ravan.

  • Time: 6:00 PM onwards
